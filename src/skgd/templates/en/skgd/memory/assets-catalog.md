## Sourcing Guidelines

### AI Generation (PixelLab / Blender MCP)
- Best for: Prototyping, placeholder art, indie style
- Limitations: Style consistency, specific requirements
- Tips: Generate multiple variations, pick the best

### External Import
- Preferred formats:
  - Sprites: PNG (with transparency)
  - Models: FBX, GLTF
  - Audio: OGG, WAV
- Naming convention: `{category}_{name}_{variant}`
